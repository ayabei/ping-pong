Let's Play

Rule of the game:

A user inserts a number then the game counts from 1 to the chosen number with the following exceptions:

  1. Numbers divisible by 3 becomes "ping"
  2. Numbers divisible by 5 become "pong"
  3. Numbers divisible by 3 and 5 become "pingpong"
